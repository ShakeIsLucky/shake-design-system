The depth engine

Built for a world with three axes.

Every element knows how far away it is, what's behind it, and where your attention rests. The result feels less like an app and more like furniture made of light.

◳

True anchoring

Pin a panel to a wall, a desk, or thin air. It stays put across sessions — walk away and it's waiting in the same cubic centimeter when you return.

⊙

Gaze-reactive bloom

Targets brighten and rise toward you as your focus lands. No reaching, no precision pointing — attention is the cursor, and the room responds.

▦

Volumetric shadows

Soft occlusion casts panels onto the passthrough world, so depth reads instantly. Nothing floats ambiguously; everything has weight and place.
