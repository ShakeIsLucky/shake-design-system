The practice

Built around rest, not retention.

Most tools are designed to keep you. Margin is designed to let you go — to finish, close the lid, and carry nothing away but the work itself.

01

One refresh a day

The page updates at dawn and holds steady. No live feeds, no infinite tail. What you opened this morning is what waits when you return.

02

Ink, not light

A near-monochrome surface tuned for long sessions. Soft graphite on paper, one muted sage for the things that truly matter. Your eyes forget the screen is there.

03

Motion at a walk

Everything moves once, slowly, and settles. Transitions breathe at the pace of a turning page — never to grab you, only to confirm that something quietly happened.

Principles

Four quiet commitments.

We wrote these before we wrote a single line of the app. They decide what we will not build as much as what we will.

i

Default to silence

Nothing pings, buzzes, or pulses for attention. If the software needs you, it waits until you arrive on your own.

ii

Less contrast, more calm

We keep the surface a half-step off white and the ink a half-step off black. The gentlest readable difference, held all day.

iii

Finishable by design

A page has an end. We do not manufacture a bottom you can never reach. You are allowed to be done.

iv

Leave nothing behind

No streaks to protect, no habit loops to feed. When you close Margin, you owe it nothing.
