The Press Process

Layer 01

Separate the inks

Every screen is split into channels — pink, blue, key. Each gets its own drum, its own grain, its own slightly wrong alignment. That gap is the soul.

Layer 02
▦

Halftone everything

Gradients become dot fields. Photos become screens. We trade smoothness for texture you can almost feel under your thumb on the trackpad.

Layer 03
✦

Overprint & pull

Where pink crosses blue, we multiply — a third muddy color blooms for free. No gloss, no glass, no blur. Just stacked, honest pigment.
