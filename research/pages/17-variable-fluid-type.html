The engine

Three axes, one living surface.

01 / WGHT W

Weight on proximity

Letters thicken as your cursor approaches and thin as it leaves. Emphasis stops being a markup decision and becomes a gesture the reader performs.

02 / WDTH E

Width on intent

Headlines expand to fill their measure and condense to fit it. Type sets itself optically, frame by frame, instead of fighting a fixed box.

03 / OPSZ G

Optical size, honestly

Display cuts at large sizes, finely-tuned text cuts up close. The optical-size axis tracks rendered pixels in real time so every word is drawn for the size it actually appears.
