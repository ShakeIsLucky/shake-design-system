§ 01 — Typography Freight + Plex

Serif display, mono labels, serif body.

Headings use Freight Display Pro (upright). Reading text uses Freight Text Pro. Labels, kickers, metrics, and code use IBM Plex Mono. Secondary title phrases go in .hangtag.accent above the headline — not a split-colour <em>.

§ 01 / 04
type · scale

The reading column caps at 720 px (~64ch at 13px mono), nested inside a 1080 px outer frame. Tables and figures may opt into the wider frame.

Body links are underlined sentence case, cream accent on hover. Strong text is just colour-shifted to ivory — no bold weight needed.

Decision · 2026-05-23

Which design-system path for the new HTML artifacts?

Three options surfaced from the interview. Recommended path is the Shake v2 recolour.

RECOMMENDED

Shake DS v2

Tight mono structure, racing-green palette, parchment accents. One stylesheet covers every artifact type.

TRADE-OFF

Pure parchment

Quieter, parchment-only surfaces — but loses the existing Shake brand recognition (racing green, star mark).

AVOID

Generic mono

Easy to ship but reads as off-the-shelf — no personal authorship.

§ 03 — Visualisations

Playful inside, palette-locked outside.
